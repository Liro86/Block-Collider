Animation instructions

Face: Click on the head and then click on the modify tab and then morpher.  
Here you have access to a number of mouth expressions.  

Eye lids: Select which eyelid you want and select the modify tab scroll down to sphere 
And adjust the hemisphere option.

Eye dots: Under the eye material in the material editor.  Then select the M next to color. 
By adjusting the angle on the U V and W options you should be able to move the eye dot to whatever
Position that you need it at.

The entire body is rigged with a 3ds max biped.

You will only be able to animate stewie if you are using a 3ds max appication 2009 and above.
